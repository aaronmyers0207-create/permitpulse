# Permit Pulse — Product Improvement Plan

## What a sales rep actually needs (based on Windmar Command learnings)

### Critical Missing Features

1. **Lead Status Tracking** — Right now permits are read-only. A rep needs to mark:
   - "Contacted" / "Not Home" / "Appointment Set" / "Not Interested" / "Sold"
   - This turns Permit Pulse from a data viewer into a CRM-lite
   - Show disposition counts in the stats

2. **Search by Specific Address** — A rep standing at a door needs to search
   "1234 Main St" and instantly see: any permits on this property? When? What type?

3. **Smart Lead Scoring** — Not all permits are equal. Score each lead:
   - Has owner name? +20 (can skip trace)
   - Filed recently (< 30 days)? +20 (hot lead)
   - High value (> $50k)? +15
   - In user's industry? +25
   - Has contractor (competitor intel)? +10
   - Open status? +10
   - Show score as a colored badge (🔥 Hot / 🟢 Warm / ⚪ Cold)

4. **Daily Digest / What's New** — When a rep opens the app:
   - "47 new permits in your area since yesterday"
   - "12 new HVAC permits this week in TX"
   - Quick stat cards at the top that feel alive, not static

5. **Saved Lists** — Star is good but reps need lists:
   - "My Hot Leads" / "Follow Up" / "Call Today"
   - Bulk export a list with owner + address for skip tracing

6. **Permit Age Indicator** — Visual cue on every card:
   - Green dot: filed this week
   - Yellow dot: filed this month
   - Gray dot: older
   - Much more useful than raw dates

7. **Contractor Intel** — When you see "Tesla Energy" or "Freedom Solar" on a permit,
   that's competitor intel. Highlight known contractors, show how many permits
   they've pulled recently. "This contractor has 47 active permits in your area."

### Nice-to-Have (Phase 2)

8. **Map View** — Plot permits on a map like Windmar Command
9. **Webhook/Email Alerts** — "New solar permit filed at 123 Main St"
10. **GoHighLevel / CRM Push** — Export leads directly to GHL
11. **Team Features** — Multiple reps, territory assignment
12. **Bulk Skip Trace** — Select 50 leads, trace all at once
